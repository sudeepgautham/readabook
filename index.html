Fictional books

“Fiction” refers to literature created from the imagination. Mysteries, science fiction, romance, fantasy, chick lit, crime thrillers are all fiction genres
A fictional book may be created to add realism or depth to a larger fictional work.

Non-Fictional

The Nonfiction books are in many categories including biography, business, cooking, health and fitness, pets, crafts, home decorating, languages, travel,
home improvement, religion, art and music, history, self-help, true crime, science and humor.

Self-Help

A self-help book is one that is written with the intention to instruct its readers on solving personal problems.
Self-help books moved from a niche position to being a postmodern cultural phenomenon in the late twentieth century.
